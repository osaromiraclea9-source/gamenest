Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the questions we receive most often.

How do I purchase a game?

Browse the GameNest Store, choose your favorite game, add it to your cart, and complete the secure checkout process. Your digital game will be available for download immediately after purchase.

Can I request a refund?

Yes. Refund requests can be submitted within our refund policy period, provided the game meets the eligibility requirements. Contact our support team for assistance.

How do I create an account?

Click the Login button in the navigation bar and choose Create Account. Fill in your details, verify your email address, and you'll be ready to start shopping.

How do I download my purchased games?

After completing your purchase, visit your account dashboard or order history. Every purchased game includes an instant download button or digital activation key.

What payment methods do you accept?

GameNest supports major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment options, making it easy to complete your purchase safely.
